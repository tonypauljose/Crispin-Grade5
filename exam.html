📝 Exam Mode

Pick a chapter, take a 30-minute timed exam. The score is sent to your parent's email at the end.

📋 Rules

  • 25 questions, mixed types (multiple-choice, true/false, fill-in-the-blank).
  • Hard timer: 30 minutes. Auto-submits at 0:00.
  • You can skip a question, but you can't go back to it.
  • Hold an answer in your head before clicking — once submitted, it's locked.
  • At the end, results + a review of every question are shown and emailed to your parent.
